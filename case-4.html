About

Concerts end. Spaces empty. The experience doesn't have to. I work with experimental events spanning physical and virtual spaces, forming a continuous environment where reality and virtuality amplify each other.

Role

In physical settings, I work with concepts, locations, visual language, and artist collaboration. In virtual environments, I build persistent spaces. This includes 3D worlds, virtual venues, and post-event narratives.

One side of the screen

DIY events, centered on experimental music and art, built with local communities and shaped by specific places. For example, the last event held in a medieval French church.

Some events are charity-driven, where I work on the concepts and visual direction closely with invited artists. More personal stories about this project are availiable in DJ Mag and Resident Advisor.
