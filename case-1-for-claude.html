About the project

Behind every door, handle, and lock in Leroy Merlin's catalog is a graph of connections: analogs, replacements, bundles, and services, spread across tools, each with its own logic.

Design role

So my role focused on working with this logic through system behavior and interface rules: from defining constraints → to how connections are created, edited, and reviewed in the interface.
